Frequently Asked Questions

How does 15 Days Trial Work?

You get access of all the features under the choosen plan. During this period you can upgrade at any time and you credit card won't be charged in the trial period.

Why do you need credit card for free trial?

When you start a free trial we need your credit card/ payment info to ensure the user identity. You won't get charged without your permission. So, take a trial with free mind.

How do you calculate no. of conversations?

No. of conversations are no. of visitors you interact with them. if a visitor talks to you multiple times in a month then we calculate it as 1. If you have 200 chatting visitors then no. of conversatitions will be 200 for that month.
